FROM THE FOUNDERS EST. 2026

"Motion Desk started as a question: what would it look like if a media platform refused to be just one thing?"

We didn't want to build another publication. We wanted to build a structure — one where journalism, creative strategy, long-form thinking, and operations could each have their own identity, their own voice, their own room to breathe. Four wings. One roof.

This is still early. We're two people with a lot of ambition and a very clear sense of what we're building toward. If that sounds like something you want to be part of — we're leaving the door open.

Shaurya Seth

FOUNDER & EDITOR-IN-CHIEF

Shaurya is a first-year Applied Psychology student at Amity University, Lucknow, with a deep interest in human behaviour, media ecosystems, and how narratives shape public perception. He founded Motion Desk as an independent investigative journalism initiative — designing its full editorial structure and newsroom architecture from the ground up. He also leads Coriolis, a student-led media and writing community for young journalists. Former President of his school's Lexicography Club, active MUN participant, and known for a distinctive command of language across multiple registers.

"Thinks in systems and platforms — not just participating in things, but building structures where others can."

Aadya Mishra

CO-FOUNDER & CREATIVE LEAD

Aadya is a first-year Journalism and Mass Communication student at Amity University, Lucknow, with a deep instinct for creative expression and a sharp editorial eye. She is the compiler and co-author of the anthology "A Heart's News", a member of her college's Student Editorial Board, and a consistent contributor to Expressions, the college magazine. A black belt in Taekwondo, active debater, and writer across poetry, street plays, and articles — she brings a rare combination of discipline and creative range to everything she touches.
